Fairy Milk

A great drink to have before heading to bed.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk (any kind of milk will work for this recipe)
  • A dash of cinnamon sugar
  • A drop of red food coloring

Steps

  1. Pour milk into saucepan and heat until you see small bubbles.
  2. Turn off the heat and add your cinnamon sugar and red food coloring. Mix until well combined.
  3. Pour the milk into your favorite cup and enjoy!
